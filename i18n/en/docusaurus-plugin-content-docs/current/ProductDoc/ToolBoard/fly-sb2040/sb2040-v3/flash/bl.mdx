# Katapult Firmware

## Katapult Firmware Description

    <CANBOOT />

    :::tip

    * SB2040_V3 supports CAN and RS232
    * Here, only CAN firmware is provided with 500K and 1M rates
    :::

## Entering Flash Mode

    :::tip

    Left is the `RESET button`, right is the `BOOT button`

    :::

    * There are two methods to enter flash mode
    * First: With the motherboard completely powered off, press and hold the BOOT button, then connect the data cable to the motherboard and your device
    * Second: After connecting the data cable to the motherboard and your device, press and hold the BOOT button, then press the RST button, release the RST button, and finally release the BOOT button
        <ImageView image={require('@site/docs/ProductDoc/ToolBoard/fly-sb2040/sb2040-v3/flash/img/boot.webp').default} size="100%" align="center" />
